Prime Defenders Academy: Origins

Established in secrecy during the First Metahuman Emergence, PD-A was designed as both sanctuary and crucible. It is where anomalies are refined into assets, and legends are born with discipline, not destiny.

📜 The Founding Accord

In 1987, a clandestine coalition of scientists, sages, and ex-heroes forged the Accord that would establish PD-A beneath the ruins of Vanguard Citadel. Its tenets? Train. Test. Trust. Transform.

🎓 More Than a School

PD-A is a living archive, a combat lab, a moral gymnasium. Its walls record victories, its shadows remember losses. Every graduate carries not just knowledge—but accountability.

⚔️ Training Grounds

  • • Omega Dome: Simulated global threats, real stakes
  • • Chrono Lab: Study history by walking through it
  • • Zero-G Arena: Physics-defying teamwork trials

🛡️ Why PD-A?

Because the world doesn't need more powers—it needs better people behind them.
