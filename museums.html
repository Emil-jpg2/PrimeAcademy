Museums & Archives

Within PD-A’s ancient halls lie the artifacts and chronicles of our heroic lineage. These museums aren’t just displays — they are living memories.

Featured Exhibits

  • 🛡️ Hall of Relics: Legendary gear from past defenders, sealed in chrono-glass.
  • 📜 Archive of Echoes: Audio logs and journals from world-shaping events.
  • 🧠 Mindspace Projection Room: Walk through historical memories using neuro-holo technology.
  • 🌌 Dimensional Oddities Vault: Safely contained objects from other timelines and realities.

Open 7 days a week. Guided tours begin every hour on the hour. Psychic interpretation available.

Warning: Some exhibits may react to latent powers.
