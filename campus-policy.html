Campus Policy Codex

Every hero needs a code. At PD-A, ours starts with mutual respect, intentional boundaries, and the safe use of extraordinary abilities.

Power Usage Zones

All kinetic, elemental, or psionic powers are to be trained only in designated Arc Fields or authorized Simulation Chambers.

Code of Conduct

Respect the minds, space, and identities of all individuals. Harassment, manipulation, or involuntary mind-linking will result in disciplinary review by the Ethics Council.

Curfew & Patrol Protocol

Campus lockdown begins at 10 PM Earth time. Night patrols may conduct ability scans. Unauthorized rooftop movement is discouraged unless in stealth training gear.

Need Clarification?

Our holographic Code Compendium is accessible 24/7 through your academy datapad. For personal cases or edge scenarios, contact the Sentinel Desk.
