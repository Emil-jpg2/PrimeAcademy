Your First Steps at PD-A

Welcome to a new chapter—where extraordinary potential becomes legendary action. Here’s how your journey begins:

🌀 Step 1: Orientation Week

Meet your future squad, tour the campus, and get a crash course in hero culture. Spoiler: There's a holographic welcome from the Headmistress herself.

🛡️ Step 2: Gear Up

Visit the Equipment Division to receive your personalized hero gear—tailored to enhance your powers and ensure you're mission-ready.

🔥 Step 3: Core Training

Begin intensive simulations led by seasoned defenders. Learn tactics, teamwork, and field survival. Your story starts on the training floor.

🤝 Step 4: Form Your Squad

Your squad is your family. Train together. Triumph together. Trust will be forged in the fire of friendly rivalry and shared missions.

🌍 Step 5: Start Defending

Whether in simulations or sanctioned field work, you’ll get real-world experience. You're not just a student—you're a defender in training.
