Campus Policy at Prime Defenders Academy

At PD-A, we believe in creating a safe, inclusive, and heroic environment for all our students and faculty. Our campus policies are designed not only to promote academic excellence, but also to encourage ethical use of powers, mutual respect, and community responsibility.

Code of Conduct

  • No use of powers against fellow students without proper supervision.
  • Respect training grounds, dormitories, and restricted areas.
  • Maintain civility during debates and simulations.

Uniform and Identification

Every student must wear their academy-issued badge at all times. Suits or enhancements are allowed during training but must be registered and approved.

Disciplinary Actions

PD-A uses a tiered response system—warnings, probation, suspension—based on the severity of policy violations. Counseling and mentorship are often integrated into corrective action.
